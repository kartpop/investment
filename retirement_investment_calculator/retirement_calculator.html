Retirement Investment Calculator

All monetary values in ₹. Projections assume monthly SIP compounding pre-retirement and annual compounding post-retirement.

Methodology: Pre-retirement corpus uses monthly compounding at the given ROI, with the SIP stepping up once per year. Required corpus is computed via the growing annuity present value formula: PV = PMT × [1 − ((1+g)/(1+r))^n] / (r − g), where PMT = inflation-adjusted annual expenditure at retirement, g = inflation rate, r = post-retirement ROI, n = years in retirement. Minimum SIP is solved via binary search against that required corpus. Post-retirement simulation uses annual compounding and annual withdrawals growing at the inflation rate.
